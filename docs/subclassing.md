# The Subclassing System: Breaking the Class Barrier

In the **Solaegis Build Architecture**, characters are not limited by their starting class. By utilizing the **Subclassing System**, a character can integrate skill lines from other classes to solve for specific archetypal weaknesses, resulting in "Uber" builds that possess unprecedented power.

## 📜 The "Rule of Three"

A standardized build is defined by **three core skill lines**. These three lines form the foundation of the character's abilities and passives.

1.  **The Primary Line:** Your starting class's signature line.
2.  **The Secondary Line:** Usually a support or utility line from your starting class.
3.  **The Subclass Line:** A skill line brought in from **any other class** in Tamriel.

### 🔝 The "Uber" Tier (Triple Hybrid)
For high-performance "Uber" builds, the rules are pushed further. An Uber build uses **any three skill lines** across all classes, regardless of their origin, to achieve a perfect "Trinity of Power."

---

## 🔱 The Trinity of Power

A truly optimized build addresses the three pillars of combat through its chosen lines:

### 1. The Engine (Sustain)
The ability to generate resources (Health, Magicka, Stamina) faster than they are consumed.
- **Top Picks:** 
    - **Dark Magic (Sorcerer):** For the "Stamina Printer" known as **Dark Deal**.
    - **Siphoning (Nightblade):** For healing through damage (**Swallow Soul**).

### 2. The Fortress (Mitigation)
The ability to survive direct hits and sustained pressure.
- **Top Picks:**
    - **Draconic Power (Dragonknight):** For **Iron Skin** (Block Mitigation) and **Hardened Armor**.
    - **Shadow (Nightblade):** For **Refreshing Shadows** (Recovery) and **Shadowy Disguise** (Stealth/Crit).

### 3. The Weapon (Damage)
The primary method of eliminating threats.
- **Top Picks:**
    - **Herald of the Tome (Arcanist):** For the absolute peak damage of **Fatecarver**. 
    - **Storm Calling (Sorcerer):** For **Hurricane** (AOE) and **Crystal Fragments** (Burst).
    - **Animal Companions (Warden):** For **Deep Fissure** and high-impact burst.

---

## 🧩 Passives & Evolution

When a line is subclassed:
-   **Full Mastery:** You gain access to 1-2 signature active skills and **all five passives** from that line.
-   **Replacement Logic:** When you add a subclass line in place of a native line (e.g., replacing Daedric Summoning with Draconic Power), you are essentially "forgetting" the training of the replaced line in favor of the new mastery.
