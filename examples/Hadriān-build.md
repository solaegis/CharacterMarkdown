## Champion Points

**Focus:** Warfare (damage) + Fitness (survivability). Move points out of Craft (Steed's Blessing, Rationer).

| Constellation | Priority |
| --- | --- |
| Warfare | Fighting Finesse, Weapon Master, Thaumaturge, Master-at-Arms |
| Fitness | Boundless Vitality, Tumbling, Expert Evasion |
| Craft | Minimal — only if keeping Steed's Blessing for travel |

---

## Gear (Level 35)

**Use whatever you find.** Prioritise:
- Sword + Shield (both bars)
- Heavy or Medium armor (5/1/1 or 5/2)
- Stamina, Weapon Damage, Health on gear

*At 50/160:* Craft Order's Wrath (5) + Heartland Conqueror (5). Sword + Shield.
