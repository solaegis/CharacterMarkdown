# Walkthrough - Dextera Dei: The Divine Bastion (Uber Tank Build)

This guide finalizes the transformation of Dextera Dei into the **Divine Bastion**—the ultimate "Uber Tank." By merging her Sorcerer core with the Dragonknight's draconic mitigation through **Subclassing**, she becomes an unbreakable Force of Will. This build leverages the Imperial race's massive resource pools and the Sorcerer's unique "Stamina Printing" logic to survive what others cannot.

## 🎭 Roleplay: The Divine Bastion

As **Dextera Dei** ("The Right Hand of God"), your purpose has evolved from delivering judgment to providing **Sanctuary**. You are no longer the blade that strikes; you are the **Shield that Endures**. You stand as a divine wall against the chaos of Tamriel, fueled by the storm and anchored by the blood of dragons. Your Stage 4 Vampirism is your "Divine Armor"—the more broken your body becomes, the more the "Undeath" makes you impervious to pain.

> **Mount:** The **Nightmare Senche** (Confirmed owned) is the chariot of the Bastion, its fire reflecting the divine wrath you protect your allies from.

## 🩸 Vampire Management: Holding the Bloodline

As a Vampire Tank, your power fluctuates with your hunger. Managing your **Vampire Stage** is as important as managing your stamina.

### 🌓 The "Sweet Spot": Stage 3
While Stage 4 gives the maximum cost reduction for sprint and mist form, **Stage 3** is the recommended baseline for veteran dungeons.
- **Benefit**: You keep the full **Undeath** (up to 30% damage reduction) which is your primary survival tool.
- **Safety**: The extra damage taken from fire is reduced from 25% (Stage 4) to **13% (Stage 3)**, preventing "one-shots" from fire-breathing bosses.

### 🧪 Feeding Rituals
- **Decreasing Stages**: Use **Purifying Bloody Mara** (beverage) to drop your stage if a boss uses heavy fire (e.g., City of Ash II).
- **Increasing Stages**: Use **Corrupting Bloody Mara** for an instant jump to Stage 4, or feed on a humanoid enemy to increase your stage quickly.

### ⚡ The Sorcerer's Edge
The biggest drawback of Vampirism is the **-100% Health Recovery**. However, as a Sorcerer, you have the **Dark Deal** "Uber" tool. Whenever you feel your health dipping due to the lack of passive recovery, a single cast of **Dark Deal** essentially "restarts" your heart by converting Magicka into a massive burst of Health and Stamina.
