# Walkthrough - Nekhtarhebi: The Dark Executioner (Necromancer Build)

This guide finalizes the optimization for Nekhtarhebi (Heka-Ankh), focusing on her role as a **Dread Reaver**—a high-sustain, high-impact Stamina Necromancer. Leveraging her Redguard heritage and massive stockpile of legendary materials, this build transforms her from a mismatched wanderer into a master of the battlefield.

## 🎭 Roleplay: The Dark Executioner

As a Redguard who has claimed the title of **Dark Executioner**, Nekhtarhebi views death not as an end, but as a harvest. She follows the "Code of the Scythe," a grim discipline that treats the souls of the unjust as fuel for her divine (and forbidden) duty. She is a desert ghost, a silent judge who walks the fine line between the Far Shores and the living world.

> [!TIP]
> **Flavor Pet:** The **Haunted House Cat** or **Blue Dragon Imp** matches her spectral aesthetic.
> **Mount:** The **Wormwrithe Bear-Lizard** or **Skulltooth Coastal Durzog** reinforces her necromantic authority.

## 🌪️ Skill Rotation & Strategy: The Harvest Cycle

Necromancer gameplay revolves around **Corpses**. Using a corpse increases your damage or heals you, and many skills *create* them.

### 🔀 Subclassing: Grave Lord → Sorcerer **Storm Calling** (Optional)

- **Recommendation:** Stick with pure Necromancer for now. Redguards already have the best stamina sustain in the game; you do not need the Sorcerer's *Surge* to survive.
- **Why?** Skills like **Spirit Mender** and **Restoring Tether** provide more than enough healing while staying thematic.

**Bar layout (slot 6 = Ultimate):**

Front bar (Two-Handed)

| **1** | **2** | **3** | **4** | **5** | **6 (Ultimate)** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Blighted Blastbones | Dizzying Swing | Executioner | Rally | Resolving Vigor | **Frozen Colossus** |

Back bar (Bow)

| **1** | **2** | **3** | **4** | **5** | **6 (Ultimate)** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Endless Hail | Avid Boneyard | Skeletal Archer | Spirit Guardian | Barbed Trap | **Pestilent Colossus** |

> [!TIP]
> **Corpse Management:** Always cast **Blighted Blastbones** first. It creates a corpse after it explodes. Use **Avid Boneyard** or **Spirit Guardian** to consume that corpse for extra effects.

### ⚔️ Front Bar (Two-Handed)
1. **Blighted Blastbones** (Priority)
   - *Path:* Grave Lord → [Base] Sacrifice → **Blighted Blastbones**
   - *Note:* Your most important skill. It deals disease damage and creates a corpse.
2. **Dizzying Swing** (Spammable)
   - *Path:* Weapon → Two-Handed → [Base] Uppercut → **Dizzying Swing**
3. **Executioner** (Execute)
   - *Path:* Weapon → Two-Handed → [Base] Reverse Slash → **Executioner**
   - *Note:* Start spamming this when the enemy drops below 25-30% health.
4. **Rally** (Buff/Heal)
   - *Path:* Weapon → Two-Handed → [Base] Momentum → **Rally**
   - *Note:* Grants **Major Brutality** (Weapon Damage) and a strong burst heal when activated a second time.
5. **Resolving Vigor** (Burst Heal)
   - *Path:* Alliance War → Assault → [Base] Vigor → **Resolving Vigor**
6. **Frozen Colossus** (Ultimate)
   - *Path:* Grave Lord → [Base] Frozen Colossus → **Pestilent Colossus** (Stamina Morph)

### ⚔️ Back Bar (Bow)
1. **Endless Hail** (AOE DOT)
   - *Path:* Weapon → Bow → [Base] Volley → **Endless Hail**
2. **Avid Boneyard** (Corpse Consumer)
   - *Path:* Grave Lord → [Base] Boneyard → **Avid Boneyard**
   - *Note:* Consumes a corpse to deal massive damage. You can activate your own synergy here!
3. **Skeletal Archer** (Passive Damage)
   - *Path:* Grave Lord → [Base] Skeletal Mage → **Skeletal Archer**
4. **Spirit Guardian** (Defense/Healing)
   - *Path:* Living Death → [Base] Spirit Mender → **Spirit Guardian**
   - *Note:* Reduces your damage taken by 10% and heals you. Creates a corpse when it expires.
5. **Barbed Trap** (Minor Force)
   - *Path:* Guild → Fighters Guild → [Base] Trap Beast → **Barbed Trap**
   - *Note:* Massive boost to your Critical Damage.
6. **Pestilent Colossus** (Ultimate)
   - *Note:* Use this for major burst damage in large groups.

## 🏃 Alternative: The "Corpse Exploder" (Trash Clear)

If you are fighting through hordes of enemies in Delves or Public Dungeons, swap these for faster clearing:

1. **Blighted Blastbones** (Stay) - Keep generating corpses.
2. **Detonating Siphon** (Replaces *Skeletal Archer*)
   - *Path:* Grave Lord → [Base] Shocking Siphon → **Detonating Siphon**
   - *Why:* It drains a corpse to deal massive AOE Physical damage around you. It's free to cast!
3. **Pestilent Colossus** (Ultimate) - Keep for the massive Major Vulnerability debuff it applies to everything it hits.

## 👥 Companion Strategy: The Mercenary Shadow

As a Necromancer, you have plenty of sustain, but you benefit from someone who can lock enemies down or provide distractions.

### 🗡️ Sharp-as-Night: The Mercenary Enforcer
Sharp's cynical attitude fits a "Dark Executioner" perfectly. Equip him as a **Brawler (Dual Wield / Medium Armor)** to dive into the fray with you.

- **Best Role:** Melee DPS / Off-Tank
- **Best Weapon:** **Dual Dual Daggers** (Aggressive) or **Sword & Shield** (Distraction)
- **Trait:** **Aggressive** (Damage) or **Bolstered** (Armor).
- **Key Skill: Gore (Execute)** - Sharp will help you finish off enemies even faster.

#### 🧩 Rapport & Ethics: The Professional Code
Sharp doesn't care about your "dark" activities as long as they aren't sloppy.
- **Dismiss Tactic:** Just like Karakedi, dismiss Sharp before any assassinations if you want to avoid minor rapport hits, though he is generally more tolerant of "professional" violence than other companions.
