**Talon Valois Prime** has merged with the Void. She has realized that blood and shadow are two sides of the same coin. She is the final whisper in the dark, the shadow that even other assassins fear.

## 🧩 Rapport & Ethics: The Assassin's Code

Azandar is a scholar and a gentleman, which creates friction with your role as a **Silencer**. To maintain your relationship, you must follow the code:

*   **The Dismiss Tactic:** Azandar **hates murder**. Always **Dismiss** him before using the **Blade of Woe** on an innocent or performing an assassination. Summons him back once the contract is closed.
*   **The Mycology Rule:** Azandar has a **severe phobia of fungi**. **Never** pick mushrooms or enter mushroom-themed houses with him active, or you will see his rapport plummet.
*   **Scholarly Gains:** To quickly repair rapport, take him **Scrying**, pick up **Lorebooks**, or perform **Enchanting** at a crafting station.
