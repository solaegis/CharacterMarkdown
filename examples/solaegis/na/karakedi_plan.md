# Walkthrough - Karakedi: The Master of Keys (Nightblade Build)

This guide finalizes the optimization for Karakedi, focusing on his role as a **Solo Specialist** with a **Max DPS** edge. We have leveraged his massive stockpile of materials and CP to provide a build that is both deadly and durable.

## 🎭 Roleplay: The Master of Keys

Given your title (**Locksmith**), the **Assassin** personality, and your completion of the **Dark Brotherhood** storyline, Karakedi's best roleplay identity is **The Master of Keys**.

Karakedi doesn't just pick physical locks in Alinor; he is a specialist in "unlocking" the barriers between life and the Void. He views every encounter as a puzzle. His daggers are the keys, and the red blood of his enemies is the final mechanism clicking into place. He is a high-society ghost—wealthy enough to wear the finest silks, but lethal enough to disappear before the body hits the floor.

> [!TIP]
> **Flavor Pet:** The **Abecean Ratter Cat** or **Shadowy Housecat** fits perfectly as his silent partner in crime.

## 🌪️ Skill Rotation & Strategy: Max DPS (Karakedi)

This bar setup removes some utility to maximize your raw damage output and critical passive buffs.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **One morph per base skill:** **Incapacitating Strike** and **Soul Harvest** are both morphs of **Death Stroke**. You can only learn **one**—you cannot slot different Death Stroke morphs on each bar. **Either** duplicate the **same** Death Stroke morph on **both** bars, **or** pair it with an ultimate from **another** skill line on one bar. **Default below:** **Incapacitating Strike** (front) and **[Toxic Barrage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Toxic_Barrage)** (back)—Bow **[Rapid Fire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Rapid_Fire)** morph; different base skill from Death Stroke, so no conflict.

### 🔀 Subclassing: Siphoning → Sorcerer **Storm Calling**

- **Why not Warden?** Animal Companion skills compete for bar space; this build avoids a **pet** on both bars.
- **Why Storm Calling?** **[Power Surge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Surge)** (*Surge* morph) and **[Hurricane](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Lightning_Form)** (*Lightning Form* morph) are **both in the same Sorcerer line** ([Storm Calling](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Storm_Calling)). Per [subclassing rules](https://www.elderscrollsonline.com/en-us/guides/subclassing), you may only equip **one** skill line per *additional* class—so one **Storm Calling** subclass replaces **Siphoning** and covers **both** actives (no need for Daedric Summoning).
- **Placement:** **Deadly Cloak** stays on the **Dual Wield** bar (weapon skill). **Hurricane** is on the **bow** bar. **Power Surge** is on the **front** bar (self-buff; no weapon restriction).

**Bar layout (slot 6 = Ultimate):**

Front bar (Dual Wield)

| **1** | **2** | **3** | **4** | **5** | **6 (Ultimate)** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Surprise Attack | Killer's Blade | Relentless Focus | Deadly Cloak | **Power Surge** | **Incapacitating Strike** |

Back bar (Bow)

| **1** | **2** | **3** | **4** | **5** | **6 (Ultimate)** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Endless Hail | Razor Caltrops | Barbed Trap | **Hurricane** | Resolving Vigor | **Toxic Barrage** |

**What was swapped:** **Camouflaged Hunter** was removed to make room for **Power Surge**—you still have **Barbed Trap** on the back bar for Fighters Guild passives. **Dark Shade** was removed for **Hurricane** (AoE + Major Resolve + ramping damage on the ranged bar). Back bar ultimate is **Toxic Barrage** (Bow **Rapid Fire** morph) for ranged single-target burst; **Incapacitating Strike** stays on the **front** bar only—swap to melee to use it.

> [!TIP]
> Many stamina builds use **[Critical Surge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Surge)** instead of **Power Surge**—healing when you deal **critical damage** with **weapon** attacks. **Power Surge** keys off **critical heals**. Try **Critical Surge** if you want a simpler solo sustain loop; keep **Power Surge** if you prefer that morph for your rotation.

*Alternative (Death Stroke):* Morph **Soul Harvest** and place it on **both** ultimate slots. *Alternative (Bow ultimate):* **[Ballista](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Ballista)** instead of **Toxic Barrage** if you want a turret (hands-free) rather than channeling the barrage.

### ⚔️ Front Bar (Dual Wield)
1. **Surprise Attack** (Spammable)
   - *Path:* Assassination → [Base] Veiled Strike → **Surprise Attack**
2. **Killer's Blade** (Execute)
   - *Path:* Assassination → [Base] Assassin's Blade → **Killer's Blade**
3. **Relentless Focus** (Burst/Heal)
   - *Path:* Assassination → [Base] Grim Focus → **Relentless Focus**
4. **Deadly Cloak** (DOT + Survival)
   - *Path:* Weapon → Dual Wield → [Base] Blade Cloak → **Deadly Cloak**
   - *Note:* Dual Wield skills must be on this bar (daggers equipped). Deals continuous AOE damage and reduces AOE damage you take by 20%. Refresh while on your melee bar.
5. **Power Surge** *(or [Critical Surge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Surge) — same base, stamina-friendly heal-on-crit)*
   - *Path:* Sorcerer → [Storm Calling](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Storm_Calling) → [Base] Surge → **Power Surge**
   - *Note:* Subclass **Siphoning** → **Storm Calling** to access this. Grants **Major Brutality** and **Major Sorcery**; morph effect varies (see tip above).
6. **Incapacitating Strike** (Ultimate)
   - *Path:* Assassination → [Base] Death Stroke → **Incapacitating Strike**

### ⚔️ Back Bar (Bow)
Only **Bow** abilities use the bow skill line; other slots can hold class, guild, and Alliance War skills. **Do not** put Dual Wield weapon skills here—they require daggers equipped on that bar.

1. **Endless Hail** (AOE DOT)
   - *Path:* Weapon → Bow → [Base] Volley → **Endless Hail**
2. **Razor Caltrops** (Major Breach)
   - *Path:* Alliance War → Assault → [Base] Caltrops → **Razor Caltrops**
3. **Barbed Trap** (Minor Force)
   - *Path:* Guild → Fighters Guild → [Base] Trap Beast → **Barbed Trap**
4. **Hurricane**
   - *Path:* Sorcerer → [Storm Calling](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Storm_Calling) → [Base] Lightning Form → **Hurricane**
   - *Note:* Stamina morph—ramping AoE **Physical** damage, **Major Resolve**, **Minor Expedition**. Same **Storm Calling** subclass as **Power Surge** on the front bar.
5. **Resolving Vigor** (Burst Heal)
   - *Path:* Alliance War → Assault → [Base] Vigor → **Resolving Vigor**
6. **Toxic Barrage** (Ultimate)
   - *Path:* Weapon → [Bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Bow) → [Base] **Rapid Fire** → **Toxic Barrage**
   - *Note:* Bow weapon ultimate (unlock Bow rank 50). **Poison** channel plus follow-up DoT; CC immunity while channeling. **Incapacitating Strike** is only on the **front** bar—use **Toxic Barrage** when you want ranged burst while on bow. *Alternative morph:* **Ballista** (turret, lower damage, frees you to move/attack).

## 🏃 Alternative: The "Delve Runner" Setup (AOE Focus)

If you are focusing on **Quests and Delves** where you fight groups of enemies rather than single targets, swap these three skills for a faster, more explosive clearing experience:

1. **Whirling Blades** (Replaces *Killer's Blade*)
   - *Path:* Weapon → Dual Wield → [Base] Whirlwind → **Whirling Blades**
   - *Why:* This is a massive AOE execute. It hits everything around you and returns Stamina for every enemy you kill.
2. **Flying Blade** (Replaces *Deadly Cloak* on the Dual Wield bar)
   - *Path:* Weapon → Dual Wield → [Base] Hidden Blade → **Flying Blade**
   - *Why:* This is your "Gap Closer." It lets you leap between enemies instantly, keeping your momentum high while questing.
3. **Soul Tether** (Replaces your **Death Stroke** ultimate—put **Soul Tether** on **both** ultimate slots)
   - *Path:* Nightblade → [Siphoning](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Siphoning) → [Base] Soul Shred → **Soul Tether**
   - *Why:* This Ultimate stuns every group of enemies around you and heals you for a massive amount. Perfect for surviving large "trash packs" in delves. (Still only **one** ultimate morph—**Soul Tether** is a different line, so it replaces Incapacitating Strike/Soul Harvest on **both** bars.)
   - *Subclass note:* **Soul Tether** needs **Siphoning**. For this variant, **respec** your subclass from **Storm Calling** back to **Siphoning** (or run a second loadout), since you cannot have both **Siphoning** and **Storm Calling** subclassed at once under normal rules.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **The Secret to Dual Wield DPS:** The real power of Dual Wield isn't just the skills—it's the **Twin Blade and Blunt** passive. When wearing two Daggers, it grants a massive boost to your **Critical Strike Chance**, which is the core of the Nightblade's power.


### 👥 Companion Strategy: The Assassin's Code

Since you are focusing on **Max DPS**, your survivability will rely on your ability to kill enemies quickly. To support this "Glass Cannon" playstyle, **Ember should pivot to a Healer/Support focus** to keep you in the fight.

#### 💖 Ember: The Arcane Healer (Support Build)
If Karakedi is the blade, Ember is the shield. This setup ensures she keeps you alive while you focus entirely on your rotation.

- **Best Role:** Healer / Combat Support
- **Best Weapon:** **Restoration Staff**
- **Armor Weight:** **Light Armor** (increases her healing throughput)
- **Best Trait:** **Quickened** (Reduces her skill cooldowns so she can spam heals and shields).

#### 🧪 Ember's Support Skill Bar (Priority Order)
1. **Shared Ward**
   - *Path:* Class → Playful Mischief → [Base] Shared Ward (Base Skill)
   - *Effect:* Provides a damage shield for both of you and a heal-over-time.
2. **Quick Fix**
   - *Path:* Class → Playful Mischief → [Base] Quick Fix (Base Skill)
   - *Effect:* Powerful burst heal for when your health drops low.
3. **Rejuvenation**
   - *Path:* Weapon → Restoration Staff → [Base] Grand Healing → **Rejuvenation**
4. **Parallel**
   - *Path:* Class → Playful Mischief → [Base] Parallel (Base Skill)
   - *Effect:* Lowers your Ultimate cost so you can use *Incapacitating Strike* more often.
5. **Vanish**
   - *Path:* Class → Playful Mischief → [Base] Vanish (Base Skill)
   - *Effect:* Allows Ember to go invisible and heal herself if she takes aggro, keeping her alive to heal you.
**Ultimate: Raging Storm** (Still great for a burst of AOE damage).

#### 🧩 Rapport & Ethics
Almost every companion in Tamriel will lose rapport if they witness you using the Blade of Woe on an innocent NPC. To maintain your relationships, you must follow the **Assassin's Code**:

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **The Dismiss Tactic:** Always **Dismiss** your companion (via the Collectibles menu or an assigned hotkey) before performing an assassination. Once the deed is done and the body is hidden, summon them back. This reflects a professional who works alone for the "contract" but uses backup for the "heist."
