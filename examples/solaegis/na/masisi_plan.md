# Walkthrough - Masisi: The Gilded Artisan (Master Crafter Build)

This guide outlines the specialized role of **Masisi**, the account's **Primary Artisan, Master Farmer, and Resource Scout**. As a "Style Master," he is responsible for supplying the entire roster with high-tier materials, processing surveys, and crafting bespoke equipment.

## 🎭 Roleplay: The Gilded Artisan

Masisi views the wilderness of Summerset not as a battlefield, but as a vast, disorganized gallery of raw materials. To him, an Iron Ore node is a potential statue, and a Ancestor Silk plant is a gown waiting to be woven. Following his Imperial heritage, he brings order and efficiency to his craft. He moves with a lethal speed—not to kill, but to reach the next "canvas" before anyone else can claim it.

> [!TIP]
> **Flavor Pet:** The **Milky Dragonette** or **Gilded Indrik Feather** matches his refined, high-society aesthetic.
> **Mount:** The **Ancient Dragon Hunter Horse** or **Psijic Mascot Bear** reflects his prestige as a Style Master.

## ⚒️ Resource Strategy: "The Scavenger's Eye"

Your **Champion Points** are perfectly tuned for high-yield gathering. Masisi is designed to minimize the time spent at nodes and maximize the return on every harvest.

### 🌿 Gathering Routine
1. **Master Gatherer (74):** Reduces gathering time. This is your most vital passive.
2. **Plentiful Harvest (20):** Grants a chance for double yields.
3. **Meticulous Disassembly (50):** Improves the recovery of rare materials when refining.

## 🌪️ Skill Strategy: The Efficient Scout

Dragonknights are typically seen as brawlers, but Masisi uses his draconic fire to light his forge and his scales to protect his valuable cargo.

### ⚔️ Front Bar (Gathering Utility)
1. **Consuming Trap:** Essential for resource return on kill.
2. **Inferno (Flames of Oblivion):** Grants **Major Prophecy** and **Major Savagery**, boosting your awareness and crit chance.
3. **Dragon Blood (Coagulating Blood):** Your primary emergency heal if cornered by monsters while gathering.
4. **Apprehend:** (Guild/World Utility) Used for maneuvering or utility.
5. **Resolving Vigor:** Stamina-based heal for when Magicka is low.
6. **Dragon Leap (Take Flight):** A cheap, high-impact ultimate to clear space or escape a bad pull.
