# Walkthrough - Karakum: The Searing Stalker (Werewolf Build)

This guide finalizes the total rebirth for Karakum, transforming him from a hybrid sorcerer into the **Searing Stalker**—a Dragonknight Werewolf specialized in pursuit, poison, and overwhelming physical force. By committing to a full **64-Stamina** respec, you now possess the raw power to out-hunt any predator in Tamriel.

## 🎭 Roleplay: The Shadow Stalker

Karakum no longer just burns; he **hunts** from the between-spaces. As the Shadow Stalker, your "Dragon" blood has been fused with the whispers of the **Void (Shadow)** and the geometry of **Dark Magic**. You are a hybrid of primal fury and calculated arcane precision. Your claws are coated in shadow, and your presence is a "Dark Sandstorm"—a force that doesn't just smother, but erases everything it touches.

> [!TIP]
> **Flavor Pet:** The **Alik'r Dune-Hound** or **Dusky Fennec Fox** (Confirmed owned) matches your desert-nomad origins and shadow-stalker identity.
> **Mount:** The **Nightmare Senche** (Confirmed owned) represents the "Searing" heat that radiates from your pelt.

The Shadow Stalker thrives on the **Pre-Shift Setup**. By layering damage on your back bar before transforming, you ensure that your Werewolf form lands with momentum.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **The Perfect Shift Strategy:**
> For maximum damage, follow this pre-transformation sequence:
> 1. (Back Bar) Cast **Endless Hail** (AOE damage begins).
> 2. (Back Bar) Cast **Disintegrating Dragonfire** (Shreds enemy armor with Major Breach).
> 3. (Swap to Front Bar) Cast **Shadowy Disguise** (Guaranteed Crit).
> 4. **ACTIVATE WEREWOLF FORM** (The initial leap will land as a critical hit on debuffed enemies).

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Subclass Synergies:** While in Werewolf form, your **Refreshing Shadows (Shadow)** passive grants you a massive **15% Stamina Recovery** boost. Additionally, **Dark Deal (Dark Magic)** ensures you enter every fight with a full Stamina bar, allowing you to stay in Wolf form indefinitely.
>
> **Update 49 Evolution:**
> - **Blood of the Elder Dragon:** Optimized for your 64-Stamina offensive scaling. It provides a massive burst heal, **Minor Courage**, and **Major Fortitude**.
> - **Disintegrating Dragonfire:** We choose this over *Engulfing Dragonfire* because it provides **Major Breach**. This ensures enemy armor is melted before you land in Werewolf form.
> - **Werewolf:** While acquisition is easier in U49, the core combat skills remain in their classic powerful state awaiting future updates.

### 🐾 Skill Paths & Morphing
1. **Brutal Pounce** (Gap Closer / AOE)
   - *Path:* World → [Werewolf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Werewolf) → **Brutal Pounce**
   - *Use:* Opening move. Deals AOE damage and allows for a second "Carnage" cast.
2. **Hircine's Rage** (Burst Heal / Damage Buff)
   - *Path:* World → [Werewolf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Werewolf) → **Hircine's Rage**
   - *Use:* Heals you for 50-100% of your health based on your massive 64 Stamina.
3. **Deafening Roar** (The Debuff)
   - *Path:* World → [Werewolf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Werewolf) → **Deafening Roar**
   - *Use:* Strips enemy armor and fears them, setting up your next skill.
4. **Howl of Agony** (The Hammer)
   - *Path:* World → [Werewolf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Werewolf) → **Howl of Agony**
   - *Use:* Your spammable. Deals 25% extra damage to enemies you just Feared with Roar.
5. **Claws of Life** (The Venom)
   - *Path:* World → [Werewolf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Werewolf) → **Claws of Life**
   - *Use:* Deals Disease damage (scales with DK passives) and heals you over time.
6. **Werewolf Berserker** (The Ultimate)
   - *Path:* World → [Werewolf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Werewolf) → **Werewolf Berserker**
   - *Note:* The bleed stacks from your light attacks will shred bosses in seconds.
