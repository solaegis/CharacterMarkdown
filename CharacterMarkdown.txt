## Description: Exports comprehensive character data in enhanced markdown format (GitHub/VS Code/Discord/Quick). Generate copyable profiles with clickable UESP links for abilities, sets, races, classes, zones, campaigns, and more. Includes ESO Plus, DLC access, currencies, progression, riding skills, inventory, PvP, collectibles, and crafting knowledge. Use /markdown [github|vscode|discord|quick] or configure in settings.
